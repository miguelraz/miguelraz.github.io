Notes and words of caution

The syntax and symbols of BQN is a big "love it or hate it" part of the deal. I won't try to convince you to like it, but I have found it much easier to take a silly, mnemonic based approach to what each symbol does: * â‰¡"abc" will give you the "depth" of something, because it looks like a little ladder that you descend * âŒˆ is taking the "highest" value (and is thus the max), âŒŠ is taking the "lowest" * +Â´ will be dragging all the stuff to the right of the tick towards the +, so it's a reduction * +` If you use the tick the other way, you will be dragging the +towards the stuff on the right, so it's a scan, from left to right.
